# SUB
- Subtraction in ARM has four instructions: 
	- **SUB**
	- **SBC**
	- **RSB**
	- **RSC**

- **Note**: Adding the S suffix on the end of each such as **SUBS**, **SBCS**, **RSBS**, **RSCS** will affect the flags
